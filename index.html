How to use the QR Scanner System:

  1. Click the Start Scanner button to activate your camera.
  2. Point your device at the QR code you received.
  3. Once scanned, the QR content will appear in the Last Scanned Data box.
  4. Click Send to Database to store the scanned information securely.
  5. The Status and Log sections will provide real-time feedback for each action.
  6. Click Stop Scanner when done.
